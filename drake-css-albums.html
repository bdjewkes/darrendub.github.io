Drake CSS Albums

Summary

I am a fan of Aubrey Drake Graham, so much so that at least twice a day I get tagged in some Drake related post and I’ve even written about him. It was clear I needed a Drake design project

Results

I animated the album art for eight Drake albums, which has been well-received by Drake/design enthusiasts.

Here is the final site, have a look! darrendub.com/drake-albums →

Learnings

I had a lot of fun building this one! I learned a lot about how key-frames work (and don't work) and how to make seamless loops with positioning and overflow. I think there's a lot of improvement to be had—the first thing I would tackle is reducing the amount of HTML elements that are needed for each album.
